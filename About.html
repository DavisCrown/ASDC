Who We Are

Action for Sustainable Development and Citizen Welfare (ASDC) is a legally registered National Non-Governmental Organization in Rwanda, established under the Law NÂ° 058/2024 of 20/06/2024 governing national NGOs.

Our headquarters are located in Kamonyi District, Southern Province, with the mandate to operate nationwide across the Republic of Rwanda.

ASDC is a non-profit, non-political and non-partisan organization working for the general public interest. We exist to promote sustainable development and improve citizen welfare by addressing social, economic, and environmental challenges through innovative, inclusive, and people-centered solutions.

Our Mission, Vision & Story

Our Purpose: Mission and Vision for a Better

Our mission to bring hope, resources, & opportunities to communities in need, empowering individuals to build brighter, sustainable futures we are committed to tackling critical challenges.

Our mission is to contribute to sustainable development and citizen wellbeing by promoting inclusive education, green-smart agriculture, environmental protection, waste management, clean energy, access to clean water and sanitation, improved health services, social inclusion, and socio-economic empowerment for vulnerable groups.

Our Commitment to Global Goals

Our actions are driven by five core principles and focused on achieving critical targets set by the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.

Our Core Values

  1. 1. Inclusiveness & Equity

    Placing people first, ensuring no one is left behind, especially vulnerable groups (young girls, teen mothers, women, persons with disabilities, and marginalized communities).

  2. 2. Citizen Empowerment

    Enabling active participation, ownership, and leadership in shaping sustainable futures.

  3. 3. Sustainability

    Promoting balanced solutions that protect the environment, advance social equity, and drive economic growth.

  4. 4. Resilience

    Building community capacity to withstand and recover from climate, social, and economic shocks.

  5. 5. Collaboration

    Fostering partnerships with government, civil society, private sector, and communities to amplify impact.

Our focused Program Areas

Water, Hygiene and sanitation promotion

Water is life. We make sure that our communities have access to clean and safe water, adequate sanitation facilities, and promote good hygiene practices to improve health and well-being.

Smart Agriculture & Food Security

We aimed at supporting and training smallholder farmers across the country with practices adapted to climate change, and improve agricultural productivity with innovation and driven technology while safeguarding the environment.

Capacity building and Adoption of Clean Energy

ASDC helps communities build capacity and adopt renewable energy, reducing emissions and fostering sustainable development.

Inclusive Education, Health & Child Protection

We promote inclusive education for all especially vulnerable and minority children combat youth alcohol and drug abuse, and protect children from violence, abuse, and neglect.

Waste Management & Circular Economy

Promoting smart waste reduction, recycling, and resource recovery to protect ecosystems while promoting green technology and creating green jobs.

Environmental Protection & Climate Action

We conserve natural resources and ecosystems while helping communities adapt to climate change through programs that protect biodiversity and promote sustainable coexistence with nature.

Youth, Women & Disability Inclusion

ASDC champions the rights of women, persons with disabilities, and marginalized groups, promoting health, fighting gender-based violence, and fostering financial literacy to empower vulnerable communities.

What Makes ASDC Different?

We combine climate solutions with citizen welfare, blending technology, innovation, and inclusiveness to build resilient communities. Our integrated approach ensures that our actions do not only protect the environment, but also create opportunities, improves livelihoods, and drives sustainable development within the community.

ASDC is committed to advancing sustainable development and citizen welfare through integrated action.
